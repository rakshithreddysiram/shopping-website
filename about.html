Our Story of Passion and Tradition

Founded in 2010, Elegant Sarees began as a small family-owned boutique in the heart of textile city. What started as a passion project by our founder, Priya Sharma, has now grown into one of India's most trusted online destinations for premium sarees.

Our journey is rooted in a deep appreciation for India's rich textile heritage and a commitment to bringing the finest handcrafted sarees to women around the world. Each saree in our collection tells a story of skilled artisans, ancient techniques, and cultural significance.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate India's rich textile heritage by curating the finest handcrafted sarees and making them accessible to women worldwide through a seamless online shopping experience.

We are committed to supporting traditional artisans and weaving communities by providing them fair compensation and sustainable livelihoods, ensuring that ancient crafts continue to thrive in the modern world.

Our Vision

To become the global ambassador of Indian sarees, recognized for our uncompromising quality, authentic craftsmanship, and customer-centric approach.

We envision a world where the art of saree weaving is cherished and preserved, where every woman can experience the elegance and cultural richness of a handcrafted Indian saree, regardless of where she lives.

Our Journey

From a small boutique to a leading online saree destination, here's how our story unfolded.

2010

The Beginning

Elegant Sarees was founded as a small boutique in Textile City with a collection of 50 handpicked sarees from various regions of India.

2013

Expanding Horizons

Opened our second physical store and began collaborating directly with weaving communities across India to source authentic handloom sarees.

2015

Digital Transformation

Launched our e-commerce platform, making our curated collection available to customers nationwide with personalized shopping assistance.

2018

Global Presence

Expanded our operations to serve international customers, shipping to over 30 countries and introducing global audiences to India's textile heritage.

2020

Artisan Support Initiative

Launched our artisan support program during the pandemic, providing financial assistance to weaving communities and ensuring sustainable livelihoods.

2023

Present Day

Today, Elegant Sarees is a trusted name in the industry with over 10,000 unique designs, supporting 500+ artisans and serving customers in 50+ countries.
