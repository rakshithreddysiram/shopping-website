This exquisite Banarasi silk saree is a masterpiece of traditional Indian craftsmanship. Handwoven by skilled artisans in Varanasi, this saree features intricate zari work with gold and silver threads that create stunning motifs across the fabric.

The rich color palette and luxurious texture make this saree perfect for weddings, festivals, and special occasions. The contrast border and pallu add an extra dimension of elegance to this already magnificent piece.

Each saree is unique, with slight variations that highlight its handcrafted nature. The saree comes with a matching blouse piece, allowing you to create a coordinated ensemble.

Drape yourself in this Banarasi silk saree to experience the timeless elegance and cultural heritage of India. It's not just a garment; it's a work of art that can be treasured for generations.

Shipping Information

We ship to all locations across India and to select international destinations. Domestic orders are typically delivered within 3-5 business days, while international orders may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

All orders are carefully packaged to ensure that your saree reaches you in perfect condition. We use acid-free tissue paper and sturdy boxes to protect the delicate fabric during transit.

Return Policy

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the product is unused, unworn, and in its original packaging with all tags attached. Please note that customized orders cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.

In case of damaged or defective items, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of receiving the product. We will arrange for a replacement or refund based on the condition of the item.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 7-10 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.
